At genX, our goal is to provide timely, professional, and courteous support to all customers. We are committed to ensuring a positive experience, whether you are seeking product information, technical assistance, or help with an order.

1. Response Time: Our support team strives to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. High-priority issues, such as product defects or service interruptions, will receive immediate attention.

2. Communication Channels: Customers can reach us via email, phone, or our online chat service. Please provide relevant details about your issue, including order numbers, product names, or screenshots, to help us resolve it efficiently.

3. Returns and Refunds: Requests for returns or refunds must be submitted within the timeframe specified in our return policy. All returns require approval from our support team and must include proof of purchase. Refunds will be processed after inspection of the returned items.

4. Escalation Process: If your concern is not resolved to your satisfaction, you may request escalation to a senior support manager. We are committed to addressing escalated issues promptly and fairly.

5. Privacy and Confidentiality: Customer information shared with our support team will be handled with strict confidentiality and used solely for resolving the reported issues.

6. Feedback: We welcome feedback to improve our services. Customers may provide suggestions or rate their support experience, helping us enhance our service quality.

By following these policies, we ensure consistency, fairness, and a high level of customer satisfaction. We appreciate your cooperation and look forward to assisting you effectively.
